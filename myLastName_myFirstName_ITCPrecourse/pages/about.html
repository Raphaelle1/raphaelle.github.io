About

Coding: changing life experience

When I discovered the world of coding it was a revelation.
Coding is satisfying; indeed, I have immediate results from my work.

Certainly, it's a real challenge because I must solve problems, patience is the key and moreover it allows me to stretch my mind.
Coding develops my creativity by producing different atmospheres and improve the design of the website.

The most gratifying is the final rendering when I see the result of my work and my efforts.

My resume

In 2014 I obtained a scientific baccalaureate with honourable mention in France.
On the career plan, I worked as a store manager at Eurotec.
I spent 5 months in Liverpool and Edinburgh at Kaplan International School.
After moving to Israel, I joined a MASA program for one year.
Subsequently I did my military service as driver.
I then pursued biology degree at Bar Ilan University.

About myself

Traveling is another of my personal interests. I appreciate discovering new cultures, learning new languages, and opening my mind to other horizons.
